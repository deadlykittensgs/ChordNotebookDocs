# Privacy Policy

**Chordnotebook**
Last updated: May 27, 2026

## Overview

Chordnotebook is designed with your privacy as a priority. The app works entirely on your device — no accounts, no servers, no data collection of any kind.

## Information We Collect

**We collect nothing.**

Chordnotebook does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes:

- No names, email addresses, or contact information
- No location data
- No device identifiers or advertising IDs
- No usage analytics or crash reports
- No payment information (purchases are handled entirely by Apple)

## How Your Data Is Stored

All songs, setlists, and settings you create in Chordnotebook are stored **locally on your device only** using iOS standard storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is deleted with it.

## In-App Purchases

Chordnotebook offers an optional in-app purchase to unlock unlimited song storage. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never see, receive, or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Third-Party Services

Chordnotebook does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. No third-party SDKs that collect user data are included in the app.

## Children's Privacy

Chordnotebook does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices in a future version, we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
